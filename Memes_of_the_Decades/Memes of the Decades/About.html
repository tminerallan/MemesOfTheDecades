Memes of the Decades

What is a Meme?

Meme’s are defined by their bold text and iconic image. The context of the meme can vary from funny to relatable and are normally based on pop culture references. They first made their appearance over 20 years ago and seem to not be going anywhere anytime soon!

This website will be traversing through the various poplar memes throughout each decade that they have been created in. Each decade will highlight prime example of their decades format, including JPG, GIF and more recently, video.
