Memes of the Decades

“One does not simply walk into Mordor”



A popular quote from the movie franchise “Lord of the Ring”.

One example of a classic text over movie meme style.

“Well that escalated quickly”



A commonly used saying from the movie “Ancho Man”

“If you spill cleaning product; did you make a mess?”



A picture featuring a raptor dinosaur posing as if it is thinking.

This meme typically displays some sort of contrasting idea that makes the viewer think.

“Problem?”



This meme features a face commonly known by the internet as “Troll Face”.

The face typically has text that mocks / antagonizes the intended viewer.

“I hate sandcastles”



This image shows an edited photo of a baby making a silly face.

It is meant to make the viewer laugh at the circumstances being shown in the picture.

“ROFL COPTER”



This is a text-based meme that is meant to personify the sound that a helicopter makes.

ROFL is commonly pronounced as “RAW-FUL” and stands for “rolling on the floor laughing”.
